Communication Dimension: Promote & Support Anaerobic Digestion around the World

The core idea is to pay tribute to those, on the field, who pragmaticly try to make the things going better. Without their unbeatable optimism and sometimes craziness things will be plain. They shape the world and their words deserve to be spread and considered.

  • For an entrepreneur this website is a "vitrine" where he can be seen by anybody around the world and consequently contacted for a donation, investment, lending, or skills-based sponsorship.
  • For the others those testimonies are a source of inspiration and allow some organisations to source some projects that fit there pipe.

Educational Dimension: Spread AD knowledge

Workshops on anaerobic digestion. Build your own digesteur to get energy and fertilizer from food waste and excrement. (karnaval, Insa)

Methodology

1- We organise a meeting with the entrepreneur in the place where he works.
We spend several days in the region to understand better his environment.
2- We then answer the Who ? What ? How ? of his project.
3- We make the video.
4- We keep in contact with the entrepreneur.

  • What is his goal?
